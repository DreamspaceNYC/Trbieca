Expansive and Sun-Drenched 2BR/2BA with Flexible Living Spaces in Tribeca

Located at Murray St & West Broadway, this spacious 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment offers 1,540 sqft of flexible living space, with the option to convert one of the two living rooms into a third bedroom.

The main living area is bathed in natural light, featuring extra-high ceilings that create an open, airy feel. It provides the flexibility to transform the space into a third bedroom while still maintaining a generous living area. The second living/dining area, equally bright and open, offers plenty of room for entertaining, dining, or relaxing with large windows that flood the space with light.

The recently renovated kitchen is equipped with full-size stainless steel appliances and a convenient pantry, perfect for home chefs. Both bedrooms are well-appointed, with the king-sized primary bedroom offering an ensuite bathroom and ample closet space. The second bedroom, which comfortably fits a queen-sized bed, also boasts large windows and abundant closet space.
